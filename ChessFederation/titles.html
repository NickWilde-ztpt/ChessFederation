Federation Titles

GCFCM

Global Chess Federation Candidate Master

Awarded to players who reach a verified rating of 2200+ Elo on our platform.

GCFM

Global Chess Federation Master

Awarded to players who successfully push their rating to 2300+ Elo.

GCFIM

Global Chess Federation International Master

Awarded automatically upon crossing the 2400 Elo milestone on GCF.

GCFGM

Global Chess Federation Grandmaster

Awarded strictly to winners of the Annual eWorldcup or players who reach 2500+ Elo.
