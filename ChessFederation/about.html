About the Federation

What is GCF?

Global Chess Federation is a separate world of Chess. It is an independent digital ecosystem built for competitive players to connect, compete, win matches, and earn exclusive ranks.

How does it function?

GCF runs entirely as an online-only platform utilizing Chess.com clubs. We handle the community management, record rankings, and organize community tournaments, events, and custom e-World Cups.

⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTICE

We operate strictly as a free-to-play hobby community. Global Chess Federation does not collect entry fees, charge for memberships, or deal with any cash prizes or transactions in any capacity.
