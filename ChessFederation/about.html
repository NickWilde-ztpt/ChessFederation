What is GCF - Global Chess Federation?

GCF - Global Chess Federation is a separate world of Chess. Here, you can compete, win and earn titles.

How does GCF function?

GCF functions only on an online platform, Chess.com. We organize tournaments, events and also e-World Cups.

NOTE

We do not offer or claim money in any actions related to the federation.

"Our mission is to provide an elite, transparent, and highly competitive environment where players can climb the ranks from local enthusiasts to world-recognized titleholders."

Whether you are here to defend your spot on our live leaderboard, fight through grueling weekend tournaments, or claim your official GCF titles, you are part of a global movement of grandmasters in the making.
